We introduce \emph{Morphogenic Intelligence} (MI), a neural architecture in which compute units - called \emph{Morphons} - grow, differentiate, fuse, and undergo apoptosis at runtime. Unlike Transformers, CNNs, RNNs, and SSMs, where topology is fixed at design time and only weights are learned, MI treats network architecture itself as a primary learned artifact. Each Morphon carries a developmental program inspired by biological morphogenesis: chemical gradients in a hyperbolic embedding space guide connectivity, neuromodulatory signals gate plasticity, and a dual-clock scheduler separates fast inference from slow structural remodeling. We implement the full MI engine in Rust and evaluate it on CartPole control and MNIST classification. CartPole-v1 is solved (avg=195.1 on the v4.6.0 codebase) using only local three-factor learning and developmental morphogenesis. On MNIST, the system reaches \textbf{87.7\%} stateless accuracy on the standard profile (v4.6.0) - up from 30\% in v3.0.0 - driven by three advances: spike-timing inhibitory STDP \citep{vogels2011inhibitory} that breaks hub-morphon dominance; Pulse Kernel Lite hot arrays that deliver a \textbf{4.3$\times$} wall-clock speedup; and \emph{stateless training}, which resolves an LSM co-adaptation problem identified via post-training diagnostics. A sequential-versus-shuffled evaluation diagnostic confirms that standard sequential training co-adapts the readout to inter-image temporal state rather than per-image structure, limiting online accuracy to $\sim$18\% even when stateless accuracy reaches 44\%. Stateless training removes this confound at source, raising stateless accuracy from 44\% to 87.7\% with no architectural changes. We document and fix six characteristic failure modes, each with a biological analogue. Novel contributions include: the \emph{premature Mature bug} (homeostatic regulator declares maturity at 11\% accuracy because dense supervised reward inflates its slow EMA); \emph{novelty gain collapse} (energy-pressure regulation suppresses the novelty modulation channel mid-training, throttling three-factor STDP); and the \emph{stateless training insight} (sequential training of a per-image classifier on a stateful substrate is a category error that co-adapts the readout to temporal context). Post-training diagnostics place MORPHON in the Liquid State Machine regime: offline classifiers on frozen activations achieve 10.5\% (chance), while a sensory-only ablation shows associative morphons contribute 35.5pp of online accuracy - the reservoir computes genuine discriminative information, but only via the co-trained readout. The primary contributions of this work are not accuracy numbers per se but the failure-mode taxonomy and the architectural characterizations it produces. The findings transfer beyond MI to any biologically-inspired learning architecture that uses homeostatic regulation, neuromodulatory gating, or a stateful spiking substrate.
